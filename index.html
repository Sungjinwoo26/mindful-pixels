Starting Your Journey:

If you're new to yoga and meditation, it can be overwhelming to know where to start, which is why we're going to start with specific tips to help with daily life struggles.

Tips for Meditation with Yoga

  1. Find a quiet space where you won't be disturbed.
  2. Start with a few minutes each day and gradually increase the time as you become more comfortable.
  3. Focus on your breath, letting go of distractions.
  4. Use guided meditations or apps to help you stay on track.
  5. Be patient with yourself; meditation is a skill that takes time to develop.
